Chapter 1: First hockey steps

On this picture you can see me played for Mshk Zilina in Slovakia

I started play hockey career in the team called Zilina and the rink was 25 miles away from my home. I had the elementary school and the high school here, and I played hockey here as well. In Zilina I learned how to play hockey and I got my first invitation to national team . After 8 years in this team I got the chance to play in Sweden and I decided to move there because I would like to develop myself as a hockey player in Sweden.Sweden is one of the best country for developing good hockey players.
